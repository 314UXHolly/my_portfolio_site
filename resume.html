Delighting Users and Delivering with Human-Centered Design

I Love People

Passionately pursuing a life with depth, I usually jump in with both feet. As a practitioner of Customer and User Experience Design and now a Web Developer-in-traning, it's my aim always, to create experiences that are enjoyable and keep the user's wants and needs prioritized. Like anyone else, I like beautiful things -- I'm an artist, too! But...in the end, In the heirarchy of designing usable things, function, accessibility, and ease of use must take priority.

I believe that accessible, diverse, inclusive, and beautiful design can coexist. There is much work to do. It is my greatest wish and hope that my legacy will be that I left my mark on it.
